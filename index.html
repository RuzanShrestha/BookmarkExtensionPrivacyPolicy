Privacy Policy for Smart Bookmark Organizer

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

  • We use the bookmarks API to read and categorize your bookmarks locally.
  • We use the storage API to save categorization locally on your browser.
  • We may use a backend service to categorize data, but no personal information is sent or logged.
